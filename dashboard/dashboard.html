REC-01 · HIGH PRIORITY
Reduce Defect-Driven Returns in Electronics
📊 'Defective / Damaged' in Electronics accounts for $163,320 in return costs with avg CSAT 2.08/5.
🔧 Partner with Electronics suppliers to enforce pre-shipment QC inspections. Target 20% defect reduction.
💰 Estimated impact: ~$32,664 cost savings per cycle
REC-02 · MEDIUM PRIORITY
Fix Product Listing Accuracy Across Top Categories
📊 'Item Not as Described' drives $169,736 total cost. Electronics alone: $106,697 with CSAT 2.72/5.
🔧 Audit product descriptions for top-20 return SKUs. Add 360° images, size charts, verified buyer photos.
💰 Estimated impact: ~$26,674–$66,750 cost savings + CSAT lift
REC-03 · EFFICIENCY WIN
Automate Resolution & SLA Tracking
📊 26% of returns take 6+ days to resolve. 'Arrived Late' has worst avg resolution time (6.7 days).
🔧 Deploy automated SLA tracking with 3-day escalation triggers. Add self-serve returns portal. Target ≤5 days.
📉 Projected: 35% reduction in manual reporting workload
